Webscrapping & Python Project

Health Wise is a comprehensive project developed to focus on BMI and at-home health care. Leveraging my expertise in data analytics, web scraping, and Python, I designed and implemented this website to deliver vital emergency health information for situations such as strokes, seizures, and CPR. Health Wise provides users with actionable guidance to assist in saving lives during critical moments. A standout feature of the Health Wise BMI section is its personalized approach. Users can input their name, weight, height, and inches, and receive customized feedback on whether they need to increase their physical activity or adjust their calorie intake. This tailored feedback differentiates Health Wise from standard BMI calculators, offering a more individualized health assessment..
